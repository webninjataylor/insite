Construction Administration

InSite takes great pride in working with our clients for the life of each land development project. Our work does not end when we hand-off all of the project approvals. We provide construction administration services to assist our clients in bringing their development goals to life, while achieving financial success and creating a wonderful new contribution to the site and its community. InSite is prepared to assist with the following construction administration services:

  • Quality assurance and quality control (QA/QC) of construction materials and methods
  • On-call services to solve unforeseen field conflicts
  • Value engineering
  • Contractor screening
  • Job site meetings
  • Pile installation inspections and certifications (both timber and helical)
  • Shop drawing reviews
  • Peer reviews
  • Construction inspections
  • Construction plan analyses
  • Project scheduling
  • Technical specifications
  • Bid reviews
