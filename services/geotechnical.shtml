Geotechnical

Geotechnical engineering is the civil engineering discipline dedicated to the study of soil and the gasses and liquids that fill its voids. The information provided by a geotechnical engineering study is valuable information for the design of many types of projects. InSite is prepared to assist with the following geotechnical engineering studies:

  • Foundation Investigations for:
    • Deep foundations systems (i.e., pile designs, both timber and helical)
    • Shallow foundations systems
  • Soil analyses for:
    • Earth retaining structures (e.g., bulkheads and retaining walls)
    • Stormwater management systems
    • Onsite wastewater systems (i.e., septic systems)
  • Groundwater observations
