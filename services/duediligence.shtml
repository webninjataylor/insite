Due Diligence

Due diligence studies are a critical first step in every land development project. Our clients rely on our due diligence studies to aid them in making key decisions before entering into a contract to purchase or lease properties. Some considerations in our due diligence studies are as follows:

  • Development yield analyses
  • Concept plans
  • Regulatory compliance checklists
  • Site investigations
  • Site inventory analyses
  • Preliminary soil analyses
  • Preliminary hydraulic and hydrologic analyses
  • Preliminary floodplain analyses
  • Preliminary environmental analyses
  • Preliminary wetlands investigations
  • Preliminary stormwater management analyses
  • Endangered species analyses
  • Highway access analyses
  • Preliminary roadway traffic volume analyses
  • Preliminary engineering analyses
  • Utility availability analyses
  • Zoning research and analyses
  • Optimum land utilization analyses
  • Conceptual site planning
  • Approval probability analyses
  • Permit acquisition development schedules
  • Critical path time frame analysis
  • Preliminary development cost estimates
