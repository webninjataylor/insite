Milestones

  • 2013, February: InSite Engineering was honored with the "2012 – Best Associate Member Logo Award" at the FAME (Fabulous Achievements in Marketing Excellence) Awards from the Shore Builders Association of Central New Jersey, Inc.
  • 2013, February: InSite Engineering celebrates its 10 year anniversary!
  • 2013, January: Pep Boys of Blackwood, NJ opens it’s doors from a ground-up construction, approved by the Planning Board only 7 months prior
  • 2013, January: Homewood Suites by Hilton, a 131-room hotel in Branchburg, NJ receives municipal site plan approval
  • 2012, December: "Holding Steady," Erosion Control Magazine - InSite Engineering, LLC assists to mitigate a structural issue following Hurricane Irene
  • 2012, March: InSite Engineering welcomes Lory A. Karakashian, PE to the team
  • 2011, May: Newmark Schools, a 60,000 SF school for special needs children in Scotch Plains, NJ receives municipal site plan approvals. Doors will open for students in Fall of 2013.
  • 2010 November: Hyatt Hotel anchored mixed use development including a 113-room extended-stay hotel, a 16,000 sf banquet facility and restaurant and a liquor store receives municipal use variance and site plan approvals
  • 2008, August: InSite Engineering welcomes Robert P. Freud, PE, PP to the team
  • 2007, April: InSite Engineering establishes its offices in Wall Township, NJ
  • 2005, April: "System Profile: Under the Microscope," OnSite Installer Magazine - InSite Engineering, LLC receives national recognition for a challenging onsite wastewater system design
  • 2003, February: InSite Engineering, LLC was founded by Jason L. Fichter, PE, PP and Jonathan W. Ker, PE
